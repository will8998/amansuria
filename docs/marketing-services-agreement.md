## 2. SCOPE OF SERVICES

The Marketer agrees to provide the following **marketing services** for the purpose of finding a buyer for the Property:

(a) Create and maintain a dedicated property marketing website
(b) List the Property on property portals (PropertyGuru, EdgeProp, iProperty, Mudah.my)
(c) Conduct social media marketing campaigns
(d) Coordinate professional photography, videography, and virtual staging
(e) Conduct targeted buyer outreach
(f) Organise and manage property viewings and open house events
(g) Manage all buyer enquiries and maintain a Viewing Register
(h) Provide weekly progress reports to the Owner
(i) Assist in price negotiations between the Owner and prospective buyers

**For the avoidance of doubt**, the Marketer is NOT acting as a registered estate agent or real estate negotiator under the Valuers, Appraisers, Estate Agents and Property Managers Act 1981 (Act 242). The Marketer provides marketing and facilitation services only.

---

## 3. OWNER'S AUTHORITY AND CONTROL

(a) The Owner retains **full and sole authority** over all decisions relating to the sale of the Property, including but not limited to: the final sale price, the selection of the buyer, and the terms of the Sale and Purchase Agreement (SPA).

(b) The Marketer has **no authority** to sign any document, accept any offer, collect any deposit, or enter into any agreement on behalf of the Owner.

(c) The Owner shall engage their own property lawyer to handle all legal aspects of the sale including the drafting of the SPA, title transfer, and receipt of sale proceeds.

---

## 4. MARKETING SUCCESS FEE

### 4.1 Base Fee
Upon successful completion of the sale, the Owner agrees to pay the Marketer a Marketing Success Fee of **two percent (2%) of the actual sale price** of the Property.

### 4.2 Performance Bonus
In addition to the Base Fee, if the Property is sold at a price exceeding RM6,500,000, the Marketer shall receive **fifty percent (50%) of the amount exceeding RM6,500,000** as a Performance Bonus.

### 4.3 Examples

| Sale Price | Base Fee (2%) | Performance Bonus | Total Fee | Owner Receives |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| RM6,500,000 | RM130,000 | Nil | RM130,000 | RM6,370,000 |
| RM6,800,000 | RM136,000 | RM150,000 | RM286,000 | RM6,514,000 |
| RM7,000,000 | RM140,000 | RM250,000 | RM390,000 | RM6,610,000 |

### 4.4 Payment Trigger
The Marketing Success Fee shall become due and payable **only after** both of the following conditions are met:

(a) The Sale and Purchase Agreement (SPA) has been duly executed by both the Owner and the buyer; **AND**

(b) The Owner has received the **full sale proceeds** (including the balance purchase price from the buyer or buyer's financier) into the Owner's designated bank account or solicitor's client account.

### 4.5 No Sale, No Fee
If the Property is not sold during the term of this Agreement (or any extension thereof), **no fee shall be payable** to the Marketer. The Marketer bears all risk.

### 4.6 Marketing Expenses
The Marketer shall bear all marketing expenses including but not limited to: website hosting, portal listing fees, photography, videography, virtual staging, and printed materials. The Owner is not required to reimburse any marketing expenses.

---

## 5. VIEWING REGISTER

(a) The Marketer shall maintain a **Viewing Register** documenting every prospective buyer introduced to the Property, including: the buyer's name, contact information, date of introduction, and date(s) of viewing.

(b) The Viewing Register shall be shared with the Owner on a weekly basis and shall serve as a record of buyers introduced by the Marketer.

(c) Both parties agree that the Viewing Register, together with supporting evidence (WhatsApp messages, emails, viewing records), shall constitute proof of buyer introduction by the Marketer.

---

## 6. TAIL PROTECTION

If this Agreement is terminated by either party, and a buyer **who was introduced by the Marketer** (as recorded in the Viewing Register) subsequently completes the purchase of the Property within **ninety (90) days** of the termination date, the Marketing Success Fee as set out in Clause 4 shall remain payable to the Marketer.

---

## 7. TERM AND TERMINATION

(a) This Agreement shall commence on the date of signing and shall remain in force for an initial period of **six (6) months**.

(b) Either party may terminate this Agreement by giving the other party **fourteen (14) days written notice** (email or WhatsApp message shall constitute sufficient written notice).

(c) Upon termination, the Marketer shall:
   - Remove all marketing materials and listings within 7 days
   - Provide a final Viewing Register to the Owner
   - Cease all marketing activities

(d) Termination does not affect the Tail Protection provision in Clause 6.

---

## 8. RELATIONSHIP OF PARTIES

(a) Nothing in this Agreement creates an employer-employee, partnership, or agency relationship between the parties.

(b) The Marketer acts as an **independent service provider** providing marketing services.

(c) The Marketer shall not represent themselves as the Owner's agent, representative, or employee to any third party.

---

## 9. CONFIDENTIALITY

The Marketer shall keep confidential all information relating to the Owner's personal and financial affairs, the terms of this Agreement, and the reasons for selling the Property. This obligation survives termination of this Agreement.

---

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of **Malaysia**. Any disputes arising from this Agreement shall be subject to the jurisdiction of the Malaysian courts.
